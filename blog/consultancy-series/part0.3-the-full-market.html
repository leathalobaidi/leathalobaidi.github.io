Structure

Turnover converted to GBP at €/£ 0.85, $/£ 0.78 where filed in another currency. "Members" and "top earner" come from LLPs' filed accounts, or from Ltd companies that disclose an equivalent partner/member figure; "staff" is average headcount where disclosed. One institute (the Fraser of Allander Institute, structure "Other") sits outside both the LLP and Ltd filter chips and is reachable via "All". Firms filing under the small-company exemption (e.g. Cebr) or as overseas/sub-brand entities (BDO, Deloitte, PwC, Grant Thornton, RSM economics arms, PA Consulting, E.CA, Fraser of Allander) disclose no turnover and several no headcount — they remain in the catalogue and the table, shown as “—”.
