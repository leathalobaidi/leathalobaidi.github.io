Filter the market

Every UK economics-consultancy entity in the dataset with a disclosed latest-year turnover. Switch the metric, and add or remove any firm — the chart and the running totals recompute. Built from Companies House filings; figures are the latest filed year for each firm.

Notes: turnover converted to GBP at €/£ 0.85, $/£ 0.78 where filed in another currency (A&M Europe in EUR, Cornerstone in USD). "Members" / "top earner" are disclosed only by LLPs in their filed accounts. Cebr (Centre for Economics and Business Research) and several boutiques file under the small-company exemption and disclose no turnover, so they appear in the written series but not in this chart.
